Our Story

Where Culinary Art
Meets Passion

Founded in 2010, FURIES began as a small family kitchen with a big dream: to create extraordinary dining experiences that celebrate the art of food. What started as a humble trattoria has blossomed into a culinary destination renowned for its innovative approach to contemporary American and Italian cuisine.

Our philosophy is simple: source the finest local ingredients, honor traditional techniques, and infuse every dish with creativity and passion. From our hand-rolled pasta to our dry-aged steaks, every element on your plate has been thoughtfully crafted.
